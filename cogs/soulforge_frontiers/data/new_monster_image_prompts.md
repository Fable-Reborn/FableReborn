# Soulforge Frontiers: New Wild Monster Image Prompts

The first eight monsters were activated on 2026-07-16, and the four Fractured Verge monsters were activated on 2026-07-24 after their final artwork was delivered and reviewed. This file remains the visual source of truth for replacements and future variants. Keep every design original; do not imitate or reference an existing franchise, artist, game, or copyrighted character.

## Shared direction for all twelve images

Use this block at the end of every individual prompt:

> Original high-fantasy RPG creature concept art, full body, single creature, centered, readable silhouette, three-quarter view, dynamic but uncluttered pose, polished digital illustration, crisp edges, rich controlled color, detailed enough for a boss card but still clear as a small Discord embed, soft neutral studio lighting, transparent background, square 1024 x 1024 PNG. Keep the entire creature, horns, tail, wings, and effects inside the canvas with generous padding. No scenery, ground plane, border, frame, text, letters, symbols resembling text, logo, watermark, extra creatures, rider, saddle, weapon, or cropped anatomy.

If the generator cannot produce transparency, use a perfectly flat light-gray background with no cast shadow so it can be removed cleanly.

## 1. Galecrest Lynx — Wind

Filename suggestion: `galecrest_lynx.png`

Prompt:

> Design the Galecrest Lynx, a lean wildcat adapted to running across mountain wind currents. It has long athletic legs, a narrow feline face, large swept-back ears, and two short antler-like crests made from pale compressed cloud. Its fur is pearl gray fading into cool teal along the paws and tail. Layered tufts on its shoulders and flanks curl like elegant wind glyphs without forming written symbols. Its exceptionally long forked tail separates into two flowing vapor streamers. Show one forepaw lifted as if it has just stepped onto an invisible current, with a few restrained translucent air ribbons describing its movement. The creature should look alert, swift, clever, and naturally evolved rather than armored or mechanical.

## Delivery checklist

For each finished image:

1. Keep the suggested filename or clearly state which monster it belongs to.
2. Supply the original square PNG at 1024 x 1024 or larger rather than a screenshot or compressed preview.
3. Verify that no anatomy touches the canvas edge.
4. Verify that there is no text, logo, signature, or watermark.
5. If transparency is unavailable, leave the background perfectly flat and untextured.
6. For a future replacement, update the matching staging URL and repeat the review before changing its live `monsters.json` record.
